Meet the Itinerary Queen

Hey there — I’m Amber, the friend everyone texts when they’re planning a trip and need the best brunch spot, dog-friendly beach, or a cute boutique hotel that won’t break the bank.

Whether I’m road-tripping down the East Coast with my girlfriend and our two dogs (shoutout to Batman and Kona 🐾) or sniffing out bottomless mimosas in a new city, I’m always on the hunt for the best vibes, best food, and best stories. If it’s fun, photogenic, and doesn’t totally suck your wallet dry — I’m there.

People kept asking for recommendations, so I decided to spill all the tea here. From fun local date nights to splurge-worthy escapes, this is where you’ll find all my travel recaps, tips, photo dumps, and maybe a few chaotic detours along the way.

Welcome to WheresMyItinerary. Pack a bag, grab your pup (maybe), and let’s do it for the plot.
